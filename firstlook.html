XOUL'S ERA

HD-2D MMORPG

Xoul's Era combines 2D pixel art sprites with 3D environments to create a unique visual style reminiscent of classic RPGs but with modern lighting and effects.

Massive Persistent World

Explore four distinct kingdoms in a shared online world with thousands of other players. Each region has its unique challenges, creatures, and quests.

Game Model

Action Combat

Real-time combat system combining target-lock and non-target mechanics. Actively dodge, block, and use skills in dynamic battles.

Group Content

Team up for dungeons, raids, world bosses and dynamic events. Coordinate with other players to overcome challenging content.

Character Progression

Level up, unlock skills, gather equipment, and specialize your character through extensive talent trees and gear customization.

Social Features

Form parties, join guilds, chat through multiple channels, maintain friend lists, and trade directly with other players or through the auction house.

Game Economy

Gather resources, craft weapons/armor, brew potions, cook food, and engage in a player-driven economy with multiple currencies for different purposes.

Marksman

Inspired by Centaurs. Specializes in ranged attacks, uses bows, and may have pet/spirit or trap-related abilities.

Guardian

Inspired by Holy Knights. Specializes in melee combat, high defense (potential tank role), uses swords/shields or other melee weapons.

Elementalist

Inspired by Wizards. Specializes in ranged/AoE magic, uses elements (Fire, Ice, Lightning...), may have DPS or support roles.

Character Customization

Each class offers extensive customization through skill trees, equipment choices, and appearance options. Create a unique character that fits your playstyle and aesthetic preferences.

Xylos

A mystical forest realm filled with strange creatures and ancient magic. Dense canopies, mysterious ruins, and bioluminescent flora create a magical atmosphere.

Origin

Northern frozen lands where ancient mysteries are buried beneath the ice. Snow-covered mountains, frozen lakes, and aurora-lit skies define this harsh but beautiful realm.

Leto

Vibrant grassy plains and magnificent cities of light. Rolling meadows, majestic castles, and bustling settlements make this the most civilized of the kingdoms.

Uranastra

Harsh desert hiding ancient treasures and oases of life. Vast sand dunes, hidden temples, and scorching heat create a challenging environment for even the most seasoned adventurers.

The Story

The mysterious Godslayer Pillars have appeared across the four kingdoms, altering the world and its inhabitants. Your journey will uncover their origin and purpose as the fate of the realms hangs in the balance.
